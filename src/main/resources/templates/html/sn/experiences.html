Trin Media (Website)

Trin Media ikambani ine hunyanzvi pakugadzira tariro ezviro zvinoshanda mumacomputer kuti vaku batsirei kutengesa zvamuno tengesa nehuwando hwazvo uye zve vachiwedzera kufara kwevatengi.

Mukugadzira tariro yewebusaiti yeTrin Media ndaka shandisa Bootstrap, HTML, JavaScript, neCSS. Pakugadzira computer unobatanidzwa nedandemutande wemawebusaiti ndaka shandisa Spring Boot uye zve ndichi shandisa mutauro wemacomputer unonzi Java.

Madzinza

Munogona kutenga Madzinza paGoogle Play Store nekamari kadiki diki. Madzinza inoita $1.50 chete chete. Dungamunhu ano zivikanwa nekwaanobva. Nhoroondo yako, nenhoroondo yemutupo wako ndiko kwaunobva. Madzinza yaka komberedza mitupo yose inowanikwa muZimbabwe. Haungati uri muZimba usinga zive nhoroondo yako nenhoroondo yenyika yako.

Basa rangu rekutanga randaka zvitumidza kugadzira, ishandamurunhare inoyi inonzi Madzinza. Matariro, maitiro eshandamurunhare, kushandura mashoko uye zve nekunyora mashoko anoverengwa mushandamurunhare akaitwa neni ndega. Ndanga ndashungurudzwa nekushaya ruzivo rwekwandinobva. Sekudaro ndakazvipa basa rekutsvaga nhoroondo yeZimbabwe uye zve, kunyatso tsvagurudza kuti ruzivo rwacho ndere mandorokwati here. Ndawana ruzivo irworo ndaka gadzira shandamurunhare inonzi Madzinza. Basa iri ndaka ritanga ndiri pazororo mukupera kwegore rechipiri ndichiri kuchikoro. Ndaifarira kunyora zvinyorwa zvinoverengwa nemacomputer zvisingaite. Ndakaona kuti ndine munyati wekugadzira shandamurunhare unoshanda pamutandira wemashandamurunhare unonzi Android saka ndakaita saizvozvo. Madzinza yakanyorwa nechiverengo chinoverengwa nemacomputer inonzi Java. Ndakanyora magwarazima andaka fungidzira ndega kuti vese vano shandisa shandamurunhare vawane rufaro runo pfurikidza. Ndaivavaria kwazvo kugadzira Madzina uye zve ndichada kugadzira zvakada kufanana neshandamurunhare inoyi.

Poets Kingdom

Poets Kingdom inogona kuturunurwa paGithub yangu. Munogona kugadzira madetembo akasvonaka fani uye zve amunenge magadzira nemadiro enyu. Kudetemba kunobva mumweya wemunhu uye zve matariro enhetembo anofanira kufanana nekunatswa kwemweya unenge wadududza zviri mumoyo. Poets Kingdom ino batsira mukugadzira kunaka kwefungiro yemunhu uye zve yakagadzirwa kuti inyatso nakidza kushandisa parunhare.

Poets Kingdom ishandamurunhare yandaka gadzira nevavariro yemoyo mukupera kwekudzidza danho reruzvio rwemacomputer. Ndino farira kuverenga nekunyora madetembo. Poets Kingdom ishandamurunhare yandaka gadzira kuti ndigone kubatsirikana kunyora, kuona nekuchengetedza zvanda detemba mumatariro andaifungira zve. Ndaka tsvaga mashandamurunhare anobatsira kuita izvozvi asi hapana shandamurunhare yandaka wana yaka gutsa moyo. Saizvozvo, ndaka gadzira Poets Kingdom. Shandamurunhare inoyi inogona kugadzira mifananidzo yakasvonaka setsvarakadenga uye zve ino gadzira gwaro inonzi PDF. Pari zvino, ndichiri kushanda mukugadzira shandamurunhare inoyi. Ndiri kuramba ndichiwedzera zvandinenge ndichiona kuti hazvipo. Kana muchida munogona kuverenga magadziro andakaita shandamurunhare inoyi paGithub yangu. Kuti ndigone kugadzira mufananidzo unopangidza nhetembo uye zve kuti ndigadzire mifananidzo yakasvonaka ndaka nyora zvinyorwa zvinoverengwa nemacomputer anotangidza patsva kugadzira matariro nezvinyorwa zvenhetembo. Ndakato gadzira mutandiro unobatsira kuziva kuti zvinyorwa zvenhetembo hazvi pfurikidza hupamhi nekureba paku gadzira mifananidzo kana gwaro rinonzi PDF. Ndaka nakidzwa fani mukugadzira shandamurunhare inoyi. Zvakambo ndishungurudza asi ndaka kunda.
